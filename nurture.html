The Programme

Merit should not
depend on money

India is full of children who are academically exceptional — but whose circumstances make it almost impossible for that promise to be realised. Without financial support, mentorship, and a network, the smartest child in a shelter home or urban slum will still drop out before Class 10.

Nurture Merit identifies these children — often from within SATHI's own network of rescued and rehabilitated children — and supports them through secondary school, higher secondary, and into university or vocational training. It is long-term, committed, and transformative.

How We Support Scholars

01
Identification

Children are identified through SATHI's programmes and partner schools — selected on the basis of academic potential, motivation, and financial need.

02
Financial Support

Full coverage of school and college fees, books, uniforms, transport, and examination costs. No scholar should have to drop out because of money.

03
Mentorship

Each scholar is matched with a mentor — a working professional who meets them regularly, answers questions, and helps them navigate decisions about their future.

04
Long-term Follow-up

We stay in touch beyond graduation — helping scholars with job placement, further education, and connecting them to the wider Nurture Merit community.

Scholar Stories

Nisha (name changed), 20
Nurture Merit Scholar · Moradabad

Nisha had been away from her family for three years after a family dispute led her to run away at 14. She was found by SATHI, sheltered, counselled, and slowly restored to her family. When SATHI's team noticed her extraordinary academic ability, she was enrolled in Nurture Merit. She completed Class 12 with a distinction in Sciences. She is now in the second year of her B.Sc. degree — the first person in her family's history to attend university.

Sanjay, 22
Nurture Merit Scholar · Pune

Sanjay was found on a railway platform at 11. He spent two years in SATHI's shelter before being reunited with a distant relative who agreed to care for him. Throughout, his teachers noted his hunger for learning. Nurture Merit funded his schooling from Class 9 onwards. He graduated with an engineering diploma last year and is now employed at a manufacturing firm in Pune. He volunteers with SATHI on weekends, mentoring boys in the shelter where he once lived.

Free Online Classes —
Nurture Merit Coaching

SATHI runs a completely free online coaching programme for academically talented students from rural and low-income government school backgrounds. All classes are conducted by volunteer teachers via Google Meet, with no fees whatsoever.

NMMS
Class 8 Coaching

9-month coaching (April–December) for Class 8 students from rural backgrounds — farmers', labourers', drivers' families — preparing for the National Merit-Cum-Means Scholarship exam. 1 hour daily, online. Completely free.

NTSE
Class 9 & 10 Coaching

For NMMS toppers scoring above 100/180 marks, SATHI offers a two-year programme preparing students for NTSE, Tapas, Saadhana, Anweshane, board exams and career guidance. Currently active in 10 states.
