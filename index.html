Our Mission

"Every child — no matter how far they've wandered — deserves to be found, heard, and brought safely home."

Our Four
Programmes

Each programme meets children where they are — at the station, in the slum, in the migrant colony, or in the classroom.

Programme 01
Children in Contact
with Railways

Our teams patrol railway stations 8–10 hours a day, locating children who are lost, runaway, or at risk — rescuing them, providing shelter, and tracing families across India.

30,000+Rescued
85%Restored home
